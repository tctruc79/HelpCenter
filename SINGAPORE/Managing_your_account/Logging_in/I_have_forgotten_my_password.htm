I have forgotten my password

In some cases, if you're logged out and forget your registered email or registered password and input it incorrectly. You will need to reset it.

 

1. 'Login' page:

In the Login page, Click on the link 'Forgot Password' to start resetting it.

 

 

2. 'Continue' page

To reset the password, you need to provide:

  • Your registered email address.
  • Click on  to continue.

If you got any trouble, click on 'Contact us' to reach our Support Team via email. Our Team is happy to support you shortly.

If you just remember your password and don't need to reset it anymor, click on 'Return to sign in' to go back to Login page. 

 

After clicking on 'Reset password' button, an email will be sent to you input email address.

 

Please open the email and click on  in the received reset password email. 

The reset link will be expire after 30 minutes.

 

 

3. 'Reset password' page

To reset password:

  • Input your new password, that meet the security policy in the measurement meter.

  • Input Confirm Password, must be same to Password above.

 

If you got any trouble, click on 'Contact us' to reach our Support Team via email. Our Team is happy to support you shortly.

If you just remember your password and don't need to reset it anymor, click on 'Return to sign in' to go back to Login page. 

You will be redirected back to 'Login' page, now you are able to use your new password to login to system.

If the time is up (90 seconds) or you have not received the OTP code via registered phone number, please click on 'Resend' to get it again.
