How do I set up 2-factor-authentication login?

By default, 2-step login (SMS) is enabled. You are able to setup it in Account | Setting.

To enable / disable 2-step login:

  • Login to your account. Please refer to How do I login to system.
  • Go to 'Settings' page. Please refer to Account settings. 
  • Go to 'Security' tab-page. 
  • Click on the toggle button  to disable / enable the function '2-step login (SMS)'.

 

You will be asked for password when you switch to disable the 2-step login.

Enter your password and click on  button.
