How do I manage my notifications?

To go to notifications:

  • Click on the bell  icon on the top right corner, next to your account name and membership number.
  • Click on 'View all notifications'. 

 

'Notifications' page.

List of notifications will be shown on the left panel. 

The detailed content of selected notification will be shown on the right panel.

You can navigate to next notification or go back to previous notification using  button.

Or you would like to delete the selected notification using  button.
