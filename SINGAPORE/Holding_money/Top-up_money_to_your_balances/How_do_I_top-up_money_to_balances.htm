How do I top-up money to balances ?

To top-up (deposit) money to your balances. Please follow these steps:

1. Login to system

Please refer to How do I login to system.

 

2. 'Balances' page 

Click on 'Balances' on the navigation bar on the left.

Based on your balance type is 'Standard' or 'Premium', different balances in currencies will be shown.  

To top-up (deposit) money to your balance, you have several ways:

  • Click on  button in 'Total Balance' portion.
  • Click on  button in corresponding balance row in 'Balances' portion. 

 

Standard balances

You are able to deposit to 4 balances in SGD, IDR, HKD, USD.

 

Premium balances

You are able to deposit to 2 balances in SGD, USD.

 

 

  • Click on the desired balance to go to balance details and then click on the  in the top portion of that balance.

 

3. 'Deposit | Select wallet' page

 

 

 

 

Premium balances

  • In this page, select the Balance (currency) you want to deposit.
  • Click on button.  

 

You can click on  to go to previous screen.

or click on  button on the top right corner to cancel the Deposit. 

 

 

4. 'Deposit | Account details' page

 

 

 

Premium balances

Please note down the Account details (Account Name, Account Number) and Bank details (Bank Name, Bank address) to input to the transfer when you do bank transfer in next step.

Click on  button to close the page. You'll be back to the 'Balances' page.

 

 

5. Do bank transfer

Do bank transfer to Account details and Bank details noted in step 4.

If you have not noted down the information, please repeat from step 1 to step 4. 

 

 

6. Refresh the balances

 

Premium balances

After you did bank transfer to your given bank account. The deposit request need some time to be processed shortly.

Please refresh your balances, and the amount will be top-up to your selected balance.

All available amounts in all balances will converted to the base-currency ('SGD' in this example) of primary wallet and summed up, reflected as 'Total Balance' equivalent in base-currency.  

You are able to go to balance details by clicking on the balance row in the balances.

To view recent activities, click on tab-page, all deposit activities will be shown. 

You can also export it excel using  button.
